FAQ

How fast is it?

Lite mode returns in ~200–300ms. Pro uses LLMs and can take ~1–2s asynchronously.

What fields do you extract?

Email, phone, name, company, and (Pro) confidence scores. Bulk + webhooks on Pro.

Do you keep my data?

Only minimal metadata for abuse prevention and usage analytics; no resale or model training.
